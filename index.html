RSGB Intermediate Licence Exam Study Questions and Syllabus Keywords

Practice questions for the RSGB Intermediate syllabus including: What is the **maximum allowed power** for an Intermediate licence holder? Answer: **50 W PEP**. How is an Intermediate licensee's **call sign structured**? What are the consequences of causing **Harmful Interference**? What is the unit of **Reactance**? How to calculate total resistance in **Series** or **Parallel** circuits. Which component smooths the output of a power supply by storing charge (Large Value **Capacitor**)? What is the main function of an **Inductor** in an AC circuit? What is the primary function of a **Low Pass Filter (LPF)**? At **resonance** in a series LCR circuit, what happens to the total impedance (It becomes the **minimum value**)? Which semiconductor component is used primarily for **voltage regulation** (**Zener Diode**)? What are the three terminals of a **Bipolar Junction Transistor (BJT)**? What term describes a receiver's ability to distinguish between a desired signal (**Selectivity**)? What is the stage in a superheterodyne receiver where the desired signal and Local Oscillator (LO) signal are combined (**Mixer**)? What is the primary method used to reduce the level of **harmonics** generated by a transmitter (**Installing a Low Pass Filter**)? Which popular **digital mode** is often used for weak signal operation (**FT8**)? If a feeder's characteristic impedance is 50 Ohms and the antenna impedance is 100 Ohms, what is the **SWR** (2:1)? The length of a **half-wave dipole** is approximately related to the wavelength ($\lambda$) by what formula? ($\lambda/2$). What is the purpose of an **Antenna Tuning Unit (ATU)** or Transmatch (**To match the transmitter/feeder impedance to the antenna impedance**)? What specific type of transformer is used to connect an unbalanced 50 Ohm coaxial line to a balanced 300 Ohm antenna (**4:1 Balun**)? Which type of propagation is usually required for long-distance (DX) communication on the HF bands (**Ionospheric Skywave**)? What propagation mode is most common for reliable local contacts on VHF or UHF (**Line-of-Sight**)? What effect does increasing solar activity (**sunspots**) generally have on HF propagation? What does the term **QSL** primarily refer to in amateur radio operating practice (**A confirmation of a two-way contact**)? What is the internationally recognized **Q-code** for 'Are you going to send messages or call?' (**QRV**)? What is the main purpose of the **ICNIRP guidelines** in amateur radio (**To define maximum permissible human exposure to RF energy**)? What is the process of eliminating **RFI** by ensuring all equipment and antennas are connected to a common, low-impedance ground point (**Bonding or Grounding**)?
